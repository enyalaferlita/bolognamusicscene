SOME HISTORY

Mozart, Liszt, Rossini and Donizetti are only a few of the many composers who had strong connections with the City of Bologna. Later in the twentieth century, other genres such as blues, jazz and pop arrived on the scene, giving way to a long list of songwriters such as Francesco Guccini, Gianni Morandi, Lucio Dalla, Luca Carboni and Cesare Cremonini, who have profoundly influenced the Italian musical panorama.
