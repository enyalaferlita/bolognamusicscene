Besides being a city rich in culture, history and tradition, Bologna also offers unique entertainment opportunities in its many bars and clubs where you can listen to live music and dance until dawn. Follow this path along Bologna’s streets and you will immediately feel part of the city's club culture.

QUBÒ

Situated in the famous "prigioni di Palazzo Pepoli", just under the "due torri", Qubò is one of the coolest clubs in Bologna. It's made up of three floor and different rooms with different music. It's very popular between university students.

CASSERO

Located in an ancient storage building along the river Reno, il Cassero is a LGBT club open to everyone who loves disco, fun, party, and freedom. Il Cassero it's not only a historical club but it promotes numerous artistic and cultural initiatives during the year, especially during spring/summertime, when you can also enjoy the spacious outdoor garden.

DISCOVER

LOCOMOTIV CLUB

Locomotiv Club is an outdoor club located in “Bolognina”, the historic popular district of the city of Bologna. It’s famous for the rich calendar of initiatives and concerts with the aim of enriching the city's musical offer and cultivating the local scene.
